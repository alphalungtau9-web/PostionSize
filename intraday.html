What Is Intraday Trading? Beginner Guide

Intraday trading means buying and selling within the same day. No positions are held overnight.

Why traders choose intraday

  • Lower overnight risk
  • More trading opportunities
  • Ability to use leverage

Key concepts

  • Risk management
  • Stop loss
  • Liquidity

How to Calculate Position Size

Position size = Risk amount ÷ (Entry – Stop Loss)

Example

₹100,000 balance → risk 1% = ₹1,000 Entry 100, stop 98 → risk per share = 2 Position size = 1000 ÷ 2 = 500 shares.

Best Risk Management Strategy

Use strict stop loss, fixed % risk, avoid overtrading, control emotions.
